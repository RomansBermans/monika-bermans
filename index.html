About Me

My path has been shaped by creativity, with a background in Italian Philology, art direction, and design, but now my focus is on therapy and personal growth.

As I continue my training, I’m passionate about offering a safe, supportive space where people can explore themselves and their emotions.

Though I’m still learning, my approach blends my artistic side with a deep commitment to helping others. I believe that both art and therapy are powerful ways to connect with ourselves and tell our stories. If you’re looking for someone to walk alongside you on your journey of self-discovery, I’m here to support you step by step.

Feel free to reach out to me for more information or to schedule a consultation. I'm here to help you navigate your emotional journey.
