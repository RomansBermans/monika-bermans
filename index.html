Monika Bermans

I’m a Gestalt therapist in training and a multidisciplinary artist based in Barcelona.

About Me

My path has been shaped by creativity, with a background in Italian Philology, art direction, and design, but now my focus is on therapy and personal growth.

As I continue my training at Institut Gestalt in Barcelona, I’m passionate about offering a safe, supportive space where people can explore themselves and their emotions. I trust that everyone can benefit from therapy, not just those facing serious challenges.

Though I’m still learning, my approach blends my artistic side with a deep commitment to helping others. I believe that both art and therapy are powerful ways to connect with ourselves and tell our stories. If you’re looking for someone to walk alongside you on your journey of self-discovery, I’m here to support you step by step.

How Can I Help You

Life today can feel overwhelming. We’re surrounded by endless choices, opportunities, and confusing relationships. It’s easy to lose sight of what truly matters.

Many of us feel lost or alone with our thoughts, unsure where to focus our energy or how to navigate life’s transitions. Often, we escape from our emotions because they feel too difficult to process, but this only leaves us more disconnected.

In my approach, I create a space where you can safely reconnect with your emotions, allowing you to feel and process them without judgment.

Together, we can explore these inner experiences and find clarity and meaning amidst life’s complexities.

Feel free to reach out to me for more information or to schedule a consultation. I'm here to help you navigate your emotional journey.

I offer therapy sessions in English, Spanish or Polish. The first 30-minute introductory call is free.
